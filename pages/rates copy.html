Rates for traditional art

For traditional art, I work with watercolors, ink and color pencils. All the supplies I use are artist grade, which means that the colors won't fade over time or when exposed to light.

Here are the brands that I use:
  • Watercolors: Winsor & Newton, Daniel Smith, Schmincke and Sennelier.
  • Ink: Winsor & Newton
  • Color pencils: Faber-Castell Polychromos.
  • Paper: Arches Watercolor Paper Cold Press 100% cotton.
If I use any other material for your commission, I will let you know.
